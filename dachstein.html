Hi! In 2019 I and my lovely wife had a short weekend trip to the Dachstein Mountains massive, located on the border of two Austrian Federal states: Stiria and Upper Austria.

Experience the exciting Dachstein glacier world & all its sights and activities above it all.

At 2,700 meters the highest mountain of Styria presents itself with a legendary natural scenery and a panoramic view over the mountain peaks of Austria far to the Czech Republic and Slovenia.

Attractions such as the Dachstein suspension bridge, the Sky Walk, the Ice Palace or the Dachstein stairway to nothingness make your unforgettable mountain experience even more exciting. Within this scenery, breathtaking moments are guaranteed as well as thrill with a view. Already the ascent - during summer on the balcony of the Dachstein panorama gondola is an outstanding experience for its own.
